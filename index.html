mission statement

We at Daisy Pepper started this journey with a love for jewelry and the environment. From day one we have made it our goal to find a way to procure and source materials in the most sustainable way. We believe that this planet is our responsibility, and therefore we should do everything in our power to help the environmental crisis we are in today. All of our materials are sourced from vendors that recycle gold and silver sustainably.

We hope you have found us because of your love for jewelry, but that you will stay with us because of your love for the environment.
